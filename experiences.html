Experiences around the stay

These places give guests easy day-trip options while still keeping the stay rooted in a calm Thrissur base.

Athirappilly Waterfalls

A popular day trip from Thrissur, known for dramatic waterfall views, greenery, and monsoon-season atmosphere.

Approx. 53.3 km by road

Guruvayur Temple

One of Kerala’s best-known temple destinations and an easy cultural visit from the city side of Thrissur.

Approx. 26.9 km by road

Cherai Beach

A relaxed beach option for sunrise, sunset, coastal walks, and a slower outing by the sea.

Approx. 50.2 km by road
