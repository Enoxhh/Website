Activities at Our Homestay

Make your stay more memorable with simple, relaxing experiences surrounded by nature. Our homestay offers a variety of activities that allow you to unwind, explore the beauty of the surroundings, and enjoy peaceful moments by the river and beach.

• Sunrise Beach Walk – Start your day with a refreshing walk along the beach as the sun rises over the horizon. Enjoy the calm waves, cool breeze, and breathtaking morning views.

• Boating – Relax on a gentle boat ride and take in the scenic beauty of the river and nearby landscapes.

• Bird Watching by the River – The riverside is home to many beautiful birds. Bring your camera or simply sit back and watch nature come alive.

• Fishing Experience – Try traditional fishing by the river and enjoy a peaceful time surrounded by nature.

• Riverside Relaxation – Spend quiet moments by the riverbank, perfect for reading, photography, or simply enjoying the calm atmosphere.

• **Village Walks** – Explore the nearby village, experience the local lifestyle, and connect with the culture and people around you.
